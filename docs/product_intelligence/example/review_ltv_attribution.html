Key takeaways
Customers whose first purchase was a 4.5★+ SKU deliver 2.3× the 180-day LTV of those landing on a sub-3★ product.
This means acquisition campaigns should filter by product quality — landing new customers on low-rated SKUs destroys long-term revenue.
The £124k at-risk figure represents estimated 180-day revenue loss from customers whose first SKU scored below 3★ in the last 6 months.
Cohort averages (green bubbles) confirm the relationship is not driven by outliers — the correlation holds across all score bands.
