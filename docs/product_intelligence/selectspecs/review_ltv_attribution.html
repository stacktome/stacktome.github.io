Key takeaways — selectspecs service review LTV
5★ reviews consistently predict higher LTV — £72.92 in 2024 vs £82.38 in 2025, a 13% year-on-year improvement. Both cohorts show the same 5★ > 4★ pattern.
4★ LTV jumped 33% year-on-year — from £53.76 (2024) to £71.72 (2025). This suggests the 4★ segment is spending more in the same window, not just more satisfied.
Selectspecs skews high-rating — 83% of reviews in 2025 are 5★, reflecting a strong customer base. The lack of 1–3★ cohort data at scale means recovery analysis needs alternate signals (see churn risk register).
Average rating dipped slightly — 4.45★ in 2024 to 4.41★ in 2025 across 11,630 reviews. Delivery and returns complaint rates are the most likely drivers given their high negative share.
