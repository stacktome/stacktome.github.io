Strong email engagement with healthy ramp-up. June saw 1,286 emails sent with a 60.9% open rate and 10.7% click-through — the highest click rate of any reporting period. Five new invite-product campaigns launched this month targeting product-level reviews, adding a second review collection channel alongside the order-level campaigns. Review quality remains excellent: 4.94/5 average with 30 of 31 reviews being 5-star.
