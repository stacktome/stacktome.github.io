6. Complaint Themes & Action Items

7 reviews scored ≤ 3 stars (1.3% of total). Despite the low volume, the themes are actionable:

🔩
Product Durability / Quality
3 reviews (1★ × 2, 2★ × 1)

Clasp broke after 15 days (FR), bracelet broke after 2 days (CA), plating coming off after 1 week (FR). All related to jewellery hardware/finish durability.

📏
Sizing & Product Information
2 reviews (3★ × 2)

Ring purchased as men's gift was too small (FR). Another ring was "adjustable" but opening it too wide ruined aesthetics — website lacks detailed size references (EN).

🛃
Undisclosed Import Fees
1 review (2★)

Customer from Canada was not informed at checkout that customs duties and taxes would be charged at pickup. "1st issue is I was never informed I would be paying extra for duties."

🏷️
Misleading Product Description
1 review (1★)

Portuguese customer expected a handcrafted, certified piece but felt deceived: "Pensei que estava a comprar uma peça manual e certificada e afinal fui enganada."

⚡ Recommended Action Items
  • 1 Durability QA: Review clasp and plating suppliers for the affected product lines (necklace chain clasps and gold-plated bracelets). 3 breakage reports in 5 months warrants investigation.
  • 2 Sizing guidance: Add explicit ring size guides and men's vs women's sizing callouts on product pages. Include actual diameter measurements, not just "1 cm adjustable".
  • 3 International checkout: Display estimated customs/duty fees at checkout for non-EU destinations (especially CA, US, UK). A clear "international shipping note" would prevent negative surprises.
  • 4 Product authenticity messaging: If products are handcrafted or certified, highlight this clearly on product pages. If they are not, avoid language that could be interpreted that way.
