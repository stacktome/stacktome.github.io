7. Complaint Themes

📦
Delivery Failures (wedding-critical)
7+ reviews this week

The #1 theme this week. Multiple wedding orders arrived late, on the event day, or never — forcing customers to buy replacements at short notice. Includes a jacket 8 weeks late, shoes shipped on the wedding day, and a lost parcel. Delivery-related complaints span EN, FR, DE and IT and drove the elevated unsubs on the FR/EN campaigns.

🚫
Refund / Returns Refused
3 reviews this week

Customers refused cash refunds on defective, misdescribed, or wrong-fit items, offered only remakes or store credit, with “custom made” cited as the reason. One occurred in a bereavement context (funeral shirt); another asks that the no-refund policy be disclosed at the top of the site. Continued reputational risk.

🇨🇳
China Origin & Fit Mismatch
4+ reviews this week

Customers surprised that garments are made in / shipped from China, several tying this to lower perceived quality and to fit failures despite correct measurements (funeral shirt too tight, vest wrong fabric, suit oversized). Also recurring: CS responses perceived as AI-generated and unhelpful when these issues are raised.
