9. Action Items

⚠ Priority Actions
#PriorityActionOwner
1 Critical Plastic bag packaging — fix urgently. Three reviews this week explicitly name arrival in a “plastic bag A4” or wrinkled delivery. For a premium custom tailoring brand this is a serious reputational issue. Move to proper garment bags or protective packaging for all deliveries. Ops/Logistics
2 Critical Delivery delays and missed events still #1 complaint. 5 reviews this week describe missing weddings, graduations, or departure dates. Delivery estimate changes happen silently (3x date changes for EN customer, no proactive update). Need ops/logistics escalation and better proactive communication when delays occur. Ops/CS
3 High Switzerland customs fee disclosure (hockerty.ch). At least one customer received unexpected CHF 30 FedEx import fee not shown at checkout. Need a clear shipping notice at checkout for .ch orders: “Note: orders to Switzerland may incur customs/import fees upon delivery.” Product/Engineering
4 High TP widget placement — still 0% conversion. 40 clicks, 0 reviews, week 3 of tracking. Widget fires on homepage — visitors without a valid invite link cannot complete review. Move to post-purchase confirmation / thank-you page. Engineering
5 Medium Monitor tickets campaigns. 8 attributed reviews this week (3.88★ avg). tickets-DE and tickets-IT generating 5★ reviews from customers who had CS issues, validating the campaign’s conflict-resolution positioning. Continue monitoring; increase ticket-invite volume where possible. Review Ops
