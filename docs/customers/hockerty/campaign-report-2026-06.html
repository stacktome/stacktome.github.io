5. Common Complaint Themes

✂
Fit & Sizing — Systematic
~22 of 52 negatives across all markets

Garments arriving significantly larger or smaller than entered measurements. Affects EN, FR-hist, DE, ES-hist, IT campaigns. No single market exempt — suggests measurement-to-pattern conversion issues at the production level rather than customer input errors.

🧻
Fabric Quality Decline
~10 negatives — FR, IT, IT-hist, ES-hist, EN-hist

Two independent IT reviewers use identical language ("tessuto grezzo" — fabric too coarse). FR market has two linen-quality disasters. EN-historical reports shirt shrinkage across a full batch. ES-historical reports elastic fabric substituted. Possible multi-supplier quality regression in May.

📦
Delivery & Packaging
~8 negatives — FR-hist, EN, DE-hist

Package returned to Barcelona after courier left no notification (FR-hist). Nearly 2-month delay with no proactive communication (FR-hist). Jackets arriving in plastic bags severely crumpled — recurring complaint from same DE-historical customer. Plastic bag packaging cited by EN customer as unprofessional at the price point.
