7. Complaint Themes

🖉
Fit & Measurement Failures
7 reviews this week

Garments arriving oversized, undersized, or wrong cut despite customers providing correct measurements. Includes waist 10cm off, wrong cut type (STRAIGHT vs TAPERED), jacket length, and fabric colour/texture mismatch. The #1 theme this week across both brands.

📱
CS Non-Response
4 reviews this week

Customers reporting emails to cs@hockerty.com or support tickets not answered for 1+ weeks. Repeated contact going unacknowledged. One customer describes responses as AI-generated with generic replies. Particularly severe for post-complaint resolution requests.

🚫
No Refund Policy
3 reviews this week

Multiple customers refused cash refunds on defective or wrong items, only offered replacements or store credit. One customer has submitted two reviews from the same order escalating this issue. UK consumer law mentioned in one case. Reputational risk is high.
