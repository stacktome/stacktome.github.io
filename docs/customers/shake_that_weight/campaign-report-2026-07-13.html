Fulfilment issues cluster in May (shake_that_weight). 3 of 4 May negatives are logistics, not product complaints — missing shaker, an opened sachet, and a slow delivery. Worth a first-order packing-list check.

Open CS complaint (shake_that_weight, Jun 2). A discount-code email arrived right after a full-price order; customer messaged twice with no reply as of the review.

What's working. shake_that_weight's 5★ share is 75% (62 of 83) across the two months. eatprotein's more reliable June sample (26 reviews) sits at 4.62★, with fill-quantity and taste feedback as the only theme — not a service or quality problem.
