★ Both sources

The invitation campaign works. Attributed customers rate 4.67–4.78 in DK across every month, 4.70–5.0 in DE, and campaign-invited SE customers were at 4.29–4.40 Feb–Mar when the Jan cohort had recovered. The problem is not the campaign — it's the experience for customers who weren't reached by it (organic), or for SE/DK customers facing specific logistics issues.

Three priorities for improving the group avg:

1. DK delivery promise vs reality — the 1–2 day claim vs actual 5–14 day delivery is the single most-mentioned complaint across 5 months and both organic and attributed customers. Fixing or amending the delivery promise would eliminate the most common negative trigger.

2. SE Jan cohort outreach — 16 attributed 1★ reviews means 16 customer emails DSF has. A proactive outreach to this cohort acknowledging the issues and offering compensation could convert some.

3. DK returns process — requiring customers to individually bubble-wrap each bottle for courier returns is unique friction. Simplifying this (pre-paid return labels + accepting original wine box) would reduce the "returns nightmare" thread.
