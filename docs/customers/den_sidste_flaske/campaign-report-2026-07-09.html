DK delivery crisis drives the June result. Den Sidste Flaske's "dag til dag levering" promise failed at scale throughout June, generating 62 negative DK reviews (26.7% of all DK reviews vs 13.9% in May). Almost every 1★ and 2★ DK review cites the same cause: orders not dispatched within the promised window, false tracking updates, and customers left without wine for weddings, Father's Day, and parties. With purchase-order matching, 84 June reviews can be attributed to invited customers at 4.20★ — still well above the 3.91★ organic average, but the gap is narrower than it first appeared: some invited customers who experienced delivery failures also went directly to TrustPilot to complain rather than using the invite link.

DE and SE are stable. DE returned 4.67★ (4.73★ in May) on healthy volume (55 reviews, 1,132 invites). SE improved from 3.83★ to 4.15★, though a separate product quality issue in SE (clearance wine sold as "minor label damage" but ~20% undrinkable) requires attention.

Negative Reviews (≤3★) — 70 Total

Complaint Themes

🚚 Delivery Failure — DK dominant
~54 reviews
Broken "dag til dag levering" and "ekspreshåndtering" promises. Orders taking 1–3 weeks, false dispatch confirmations, packages not handed to carrier. Father's Day, Pinse, and private party occasions missed. Customers mention WebrinQ and DAO by name. Affects all of June, not isolated to one period.
📦 Stock & Fulfilment Errors
~10 reviews
Items shown as in-stock at purchase, then discovered to be on back-order after 5–10 days of silence. Wrong wines delivered. Split deliveries (6 of 12 bottles, wait weeks for the rest). One case of an advertised Facebook offer (12 bottles + 2 magnums) where company denies the magnums were included despite customer screenshot.
🍷 Wine Quality
~6 reviews
Three distinct issues: (1) SE clearance-box wine — company sold "udrensningslådor" knowing ~20% was oxidised/vinegar-level undrinkable, without disclosing this in the ad. (2) "The Gov'nor Tinto" (DK + DE): taste inconsistent vs previous experience — possible batch variation. (3) Wine stored too warm: 7/12 bottles poured out (DK 1★, Jun 25).
⚙️ Other
~4 reviews
Misleading bottle size (187 ml listed alongside full-size bottles without clear labelling). AI-generated customer service response flagged as impersonal. Return shipping charged to customer for an error DSF made. Checkout shows free shipping but payment page adds freight.

Action Items

  • 1
    Audit and correct "dag til dag levering" and "ekspreshåndtering" claims on the DK website — The gap between promised and actual delivery times is the single largest source of negative reviews in June (estimated ~54 of 70 negatives). Multiple customers specifically cite the WebrinQ handover as the failure point, but the advertised promise is also not matching reality at the warehouse level. Either the SLA needs to be met consistently or the promise needs to be revised to match actual performance. This is a reputational risk that compounds monthly.
  • 2
    Investigate the SE clearance-box ("udrensningslådor") disclosure policy — A June SE review revealed that DSF sold clearance boxes knowing ~20% of bottles might be oxidised/undrinkable, without disclosing this in the listing or advertisement. The customer's screenshot of DSF's own response confirms the company was aware. This creates both a customer trust problem and a potential consumer-law issue in Sweden. The listing copy should disclose quality risks explicitly, and customers who received affected bottles should be contacted proactively.
  • 3
    Investigate The Gov'nor Tinto taste complaints (DK + DE) — Two independent reviews from DK and DE in June describe the wine tasting noticeably different from previous purchases. The DE customer specifically researched and found other customers reporting the same. This could indicate a batch issue or supplier change. Worth flagging to the buying team.
  • 4
    Increase SE invite volume — review rate is 0.59% — SE invite-recent (576) sent only 1,012 invites in June generating 6 attributed reviews (after purchase-order matching). With 20 total June SE reviews, there is an audience of satisfied customers not reached by the campaign. Note: the 3 newly matched SE attributed reviews are delivery complainants, so attributed avg is currently 2.83★ — fixing the delivery issue should be the first priority before scaling invite volume.
