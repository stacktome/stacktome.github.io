4. Key Signal This Week

⚠ Welcome Call/Setup CTR: 19.3% → 8.2%

This campaign is the highest-volume send (61 invites) and its click-through rate halved week-on-week while its open rate actually improved (75.4%→77.0%). The disconnect between strong opens and weak clicks points to a CTA or email body issue rather than a subject line or deliverability problem.

Suggested checks:
  • Verify the review invite link/button is rendering correctly in the current template
  • Check whether a template or subject line change was made between the two periods
  • Compare cohort stage: are this week’s learners at a different point in their journey than last week’s?
