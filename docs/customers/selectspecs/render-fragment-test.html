Is SelectSpecs legit and trustworthy?

Yes. SelectSpecs has traded since 2005 and is rated 4.5 out of 5 from 83,276 customer reviews. Every review on this page is order-verified: invitations are sent only to customers who completed a purchase, so reviews cannot be submitted by people who never ordered.

How long does SelectSpecs delivery take?

Most customers report receiving standard prescription glasses within 1-3 weeks, as lenses are made to order. An Express Service is available; customers using it report delivery in as little as 2 days. 84% of reviews mentioning delivery in the last 12 months are positive; the most common complaint is orders taking longer than expected during stock delays.

Are SelectSpecs glasses good quality?

92% of reviews mentioning product quality in the last 12 months are positive. Customers frequently compare frame and lens quality favourably to high-street opticians at twice the price. Negative quality reviews most often concern prescription accuracy on complex prescriptions such as varifocals, and occasional fit or durability issues.

What happens if my glasses are wrong or don't fit?

SelectSpecs offers free returns and exchanges. Customers report mixed experiences with the returns process: refunds and remakes are honoured, but some reviews mention the process taking longer than expected. If a prescription is unusual, SelectSpecs contacts the customer to confirm it before manufacturing.

Is SelectSpecs customer service responsive?

90% of reviews mentioning customer service in the last 12 months are positive. Customers highlight clear communication during the order process and helpful staff, including proactive checks on unusual prescriptions.

Is SelectSpecs cheaper than high-street opticians?

Yes - prescription glasses start from £6 including basic single-vision lenses, and price/value is the most consistently praised theme in reviews. Customers note that optional extras and non-standard lenses increase the price, so the final cost depends on the configuration chosen.
