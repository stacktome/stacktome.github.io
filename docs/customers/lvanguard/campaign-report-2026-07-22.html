Negative Review Themes

238 reviews rated ≤3★ YTD (Carethy 195, Bulevip 43) analysed by theme via keyword matching across the review text (EN/ES/FR/DE/IT/NL). Categories overlap — a single review can mention both a delivery failure and an unresponsive support team. Full backlog lives in Zeppelin notebook lvanguard_data.

📦
Delivery & Carrier Failures
70 of 238 negative reviews (29%) · Carethy 53, Bulevip 17

The dominant theme by a wide margin on both brands. Damaged parcels stuck in "parcel damaged" carrier status with no resolution, non-delivery after weeks of waiting, lost packages, orders split across delivery methods without notice, and named-carrier delays (DPD, DHL, Correos, SEUR). Spans DE/FR/UK/ES markets on Carethy and ES on Bulevip.

🔧
Damaged / Wrong / Missing Items
29 of 238 negative reviews (12%) · Carethy 18, Bulevip 11

Boxes arriving already opened with items missing, wrong product shipped instead of what was ordered, and products damaged in transit (e.g. leaking shampoo, broken packaging). Distinct from pure delivery delay — the parcel arrives but its contents are wrong or compromised.

💬
Refund Delays & Unresponsive Support
23 of 238 negative reviews (10%) · Carethy 20, Bulevip 3

Customers describe refunds stuck "under review" with no timeline, repeated emails to customer service going unanswered, and generic non-committal replies. Heavily concentrated on Carethy — worth checking whether this correlates with the high no-ticket rate below.

✉️
Missing Order Confirmation / Communication
9 of 238 negative reviews (4%) · Carethy 5, Bulevip 4

Customers report no order confirmation or invoice email after payment was taken, and no proactive notice of delays or split shipments. Smaller in volume than the other themes but a low-cost fix if it traces to a transactional-email issue rather than a policy choice.

Action Items

  • 🔴
    127 of 158 Carethy negative reviews (≤2★) have no matching support ticket — 80% untracked. This is a much higher untracked rate than Bulevip's 58%. Confirm whether Carethy tickets are logged under a different system/email pattern before assuming these are genuinely unactioned; if confirmed, this is a large backlog of unresolved delivery/refund complaints (see recurring "parcel damaged," "never received," "no refund" themes above).
  • 🔴
    Carrier/delivery failures are the dominant complaint driver on both brands. Damaged parcels stuck in "parcel damaged" carrier status with no follow-up, lost packages requiring repeated customer-side documentation, wrong/missing items in multi-item orders, and orders split across delivery methods without notice. Recommend a logistics review — carrier performance and proactive delay/damage notifications appear to be the single biggest lever for rating improvement on both brands.
  • 🟡
    Both brands' review rate dipped in June (Carethy 3.46%, Bulevip 2.97% vs 5%+ typical) despite invite volume being at or near YTD highs. Worth a follow-up next report once more of June's reviews have had time to land — could be normal lag or could be a genuine engagement drop worth investigating (subject lines, send timing, audience quality).
  • 🟡
    Bulevip: customer reports discontinued loyalty gifts and missing order-status emails. A long-time customer's 1★ review (Jul 9) specifically calls out that order confirmation emails stopped arriving and that small gifts on larger orders (shakers, bars, gels) have been discontinued — both cited as reasons for considering competitors. Worth checking whether transactional email delivery has a technical issue, separate from the loyalty-perk decision.
  • 🟢
    Email performance is healthy — 52.0% open rate, 5.0% CTR across 8,008 sends in the last 30 days. Bulevip's smaller markets (DE 72.7%, IT 66.3%) are punching well above the group average, worth a look for what's different.
  • 🟢
    Bulevip invite-tp-IT is the standout campaign: 7.46% review rate (10 reviews from 134 invites, avg 4.30★) — the best rate on either brand. Low volume, but worth investigating whether the IT market messaging or audience is doing something differently that could be replicated.
  • 🔵
    Onboarding note: a third sub-account, lvanguard_biuky, exists under the same _projectId=lvanguard but was out of scope for this report. If Biuky should be included going forward, confirm its real brand name and add it to the Account Registry and this report's structure.
