4. Complaint Themes

🚚
EVRI Delivery Failures
~65% of negative reviews

Non-delivery, late delivery, no notification, items returned to depot. Multiple reviewers name EVRI directly. Affects time-sensitive occasions (birthdays, anniversaries, Mother’s Day). Some flowers delivered 4–10 days late or not at all.

🌸
Wilted / Dead Flowers
~20% of negative reviews

Flowers arriving already wilted, dead, or dying within 24 hours. Roses with drooping heads, bouquets looking "exhausted." Likely transit time compounded by delivery delays leaves flowers in vans for days.

📦
Damaged Packaging
~10% of negative reviews

Crushed, battered, or heavily damaged boxes on delivery. Flowers damaged inside the box. One customer reports vase delivered smashed.

📷
Product Not as Shown
~8% of negative reviews

Bouquet received significantly different from website photo — smaller, fewer flowers, wrong colours. Customers feel misled by product imagery.

🎁
Missing / Wrong Items
~5% of negative reviews

Paid-for extras not delivered: chocolates, greeting cards, add-on bears. One customer received wrong item (slippers) instead of ordered product.

📞
CS Failures
~5% of negative reviews

Refunds refused on quick cancellations, template responses, emails bouncing, vouchers not honoured. One customer escalated to Trading Standards after refund issued without goodwill gesture.
